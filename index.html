About MUREF

Founded by Hon. Vitumbiko Mumba, MUREF is a community-based microloan initiative focused on sustainable development in Mzimba Central.

Philosophy

“We must move beyond coffin based aid and invest in the living. Real development comes from empowering entrepreneur's and creating opportunities that transform communities .”

— Hon. Mumba (English)

“Tisiyeni kutumbika umphawi ndi kupereka makofi.”

— Hon. Mumba (Tumbuka)
